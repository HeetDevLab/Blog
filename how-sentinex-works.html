How Sentinex App Lock Works

Published by HeetDevLab • Privacy-First Android Security

Sentinex is designed as a lightweight Android app lock focused on privacy and performance. Unlike traditional app lockers, it avoids tracking and unnecessary background services.

1. Accessibility-Based Monitoring

Sentinex uses Android's accessibility service to detect when protected apps are opened. When an app launch is detected, Sentinex instantly overlays a secure lock screen.

2. No Data Tracking

Sentinex does not collect usage data or send analytics to any server. All processing happens locally on your device.

3. Lightweight Architecture

The app is optimized for low RAM usage and smooth performance, ensuring it doesn't slow down your device.

Why Privacy-First Matters

Many security apps collect user data under the name of analytics. Sentinex follows a strict privacy-first policy, ensuring full control stays with the user.
